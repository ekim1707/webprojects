The Widow's Kiss

The life of a sniper in a FPS jungle

It's almost always the same....

At first, nobody even really acknowledges you. But after your first few headshots, it feels like it's suddenly completely personal...

First they start by sending a gigantic super-leaping monkey with a Tesla Cannon after you... Then comes along an expert-hacker spy who can be invisible whenever she wants.

Next is a robotic, diabolical-looking ninja with a devastating Katana blade, capable of deflecting literally... (pause for emphasis)... anything, including stuff like missiles and fireblasts.

And of course it's always wonderful when that psychopathic terrorist with dual-wielding hellfire shotguns, wraith-mode, and the ability to teleport finally shows up.

You've managed to piss them all off, and now you've got to stay alive using just your slow-charge rifle, single-use-only escape grapple, and some kind of unreal, zero margin-for-error shot accuracy that you have to pull out of... idk, somewhere...

Fun times.
