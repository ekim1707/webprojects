The Widow's Kiss

The life of a sniper in a FPS jungle


Narrative:

It's almost always the same....



At first, nobody even really acknowledges you. But after your first few headshots, it feels like everything's suddenly completely personal...

First they start by sending a gigantic super-leaping monkey with a Tesla Cannon after you... Then comes along an expert-hacker spy who can be invisible whenever she wants.

Next up is a robotic, diabolical-looking ninja with a devastating Katana blade, capable of deflecting literally... (pause for emphasis)... anything, including stuff like missiles and fireblasts (forreal?).

And of course it's always wonderful when that psychopathic terrorist with dual-wielding hellfire shotguns, wraith-mode, and the ability to teleport finally shows up.

You've managed to piss them all off, and now you've got to stay alive using just your slow-charge rifle, single-use-only escape grapple, and that small ray of hope that you can achieve that no-margin-for-error shot accuracy that's necessary to survive everybody diving you over and over again.

Fun times.
