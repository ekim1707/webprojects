Lyrical Influence

  • From the Inside Out

    Many people remember Joel for his breakout, historically-renown single, "From the Inside Out (Everlasting)". Perhaps the most moving lyrics in this masterpiece come immediately in the introduction:

    A thousand times I've failed
    Your mercy remains
    Should I stumble again
    I'm caught in Your grace

  • Solution

    This hit song, Solution, was written as Joel's response to all the chaos and struggle circulating through the world news at the time. Joel's simple assertion to the world was for the people of the world to rise up through faith and be the instruments necessary to make a positive change.

    It is not too far a cry
    Too much to try
    To help the least of these
    Politics will not decide
    If we should rise
    And be Your hands and feet

  • Tear Down the Walls

    One of Joel's later, more passionate songs, this title song from the "Across the Earth: Tear Down the Walls" album was one of his more low-profile ones. Still, the passion in his lyrics remains clearly evident, in a song addresses some of the more darker struggles in different walks of life.

    Tear down the walls, see the world
    Is there something we have missed
    Turn from ourselves, long beyond
    There's so much more than this
